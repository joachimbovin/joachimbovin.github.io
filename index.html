Joachim Bovin is a teacher, musician & programmer based in Brussels. He is currently employed as a teaching assistant at the Faculty of Arts of the KULeuven for a number faculty wide courses related to use of digital technologies in the humanities. He is interested in the data visualisations, static websites & the influence of new technologies on cultural practices. He is the founder of record label and radio show Anterior Insula and resident DJ at Kiosk Radio.
